About Me

Hi, I'm Jon, I'm currently a pro bono developer at Food Oasis LA and an instructor at Beach Coders Academy. Before my foray into the LA produce scene, I co-created DejaVue, a visualization Chrome extension tool for Vue developers. And before that, I spent two years at TrueAccord, a debt collection fintech startup.

I'm familiar with most JavaScript frameworks and tools including React/Redux, Vue, and Node/Express. I stay up-to-date with whatever is the latest in the software world, and constantly try to build things on the side that others may find remotely useful.

When I'm not working on my projects, you can find me practicing Spanish, sitting around a bonfire somewhere, or planning my next international trip. I care deeply about personal development, preventing climate change, and being a part of the open-source software community and am happy to connect with people with similar interests.
